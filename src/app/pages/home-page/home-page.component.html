MARS SRM {{country}} - Advanced Analytics Tool Kit

Pricing Tool

Scenario Creation capability to plan for Pricing with the impact of price increases on volume and Mars/Customer P&L metrics. Data-Driven Price Simulation enables buy-in from key stakeholders in order to seamlessly execute pricing decisions.

MARS Category and Pricing Dashboard

Helps to understand the price trends across manufacturers, brands and pack types over time. Allows users to identify the instances in which products are under-priced or over-priced relative to the competitive positioning and where price adjustments are required, thus helping them to understand and improve the overall effectiveness of the pricing strategy.

Promo Tool

Promo tool which enables the stakeholders to optimize the promotion plans subject to various constraints & simulate various promo scenarios and understand the impact on financial metrics for MARS and the customers.

Promo Insights

A one-stop platform to get a 360-degree view on the effectiveness of trade promotion levers and observe trends that will allow Trade Executives & SRM teams to identify opportunities to enable account teams to drive growth & add value to retail partners.

Profit Pool

The Profit Pool tool is a complete solution giving the users a hawkeye view of the financial portfolio of the business. It provides visibility into business margins and expenses, across years and different channels, this in turn will enable the key stakeholders to identify potential value drivers.
